A dip everyone loves. SALSA!

Recipe originally from Natashas Kitchen and found at https://natashaskitchen.com/easy-homemade-salsa/

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Roma tomatoes
  • 12oz canned petite diced tomatoes, with their juice
  • 1/2 cup white onion
  • 1/3 cup fresh cilantro
  • 1 jalapeno pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 Tbsp lime juice, from 1/2 fresh lime
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 2 tsp salt

Tools Needed

  • Food processor
  • Knife
  • Spoon
  • Cutting board
  • Bowl

Instructions

  1. Chop up your veggies; you must also deseed your Jalapenos if you want a milder taste.
  2. Throw all your ingredients into the food processor. Make sure not to over-blend.
  3. Move it from the food processor to a bowl and season your salsa with your salt.
  4. Place the bowl in the refrigerator to marinate for two hours or overnight.
  5. Enjoy!
